No Griefing: Do not intentionally destroy or modify other players' builds without their permission.

Respect Others: Treat all players with respect and refrain from using offensive language or behavior.

No Cheating or Hacking: Any form of cheating or hacking, including the use of modified clients, x-ray texture packs, or exploits, is strictly prohibited.

No Stealing: Do not take items or resources from other players without their consent.

Build Responsibly: Construct your builds in appropriate locations, avoiding obstructing others' projects or important server infrastructure.

No PvP without Consent: PvP (Player versus Player combat) is only allowed if both parties agree to it. Non-consensual PvP is not permitted.

Follow Staff Instructions: Respect and comply with the instructions of server staff members. They are there to ensure fair play and a positive gaming experience for everyone.

No Spamming or Advertising: Avoid excessive chat spamming, and do not advertise other servers or websites without permission.

Keep Redstone Lag to a Minimum: Redstone contraptions that cause excessive lag or strain on the server are discouraged. Optimize your designs to minimize server performance issues.

Report Issues: If you encounter any bugs, glitches, or rule violations, report them to server staff promptly and responsibly.
